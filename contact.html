About Fox Valley Realty

Fox Valley Realty is a locally trusted real estate agency dedicated to helping individuals and families find their perfect home in the heart of Wisconsin’s Fox Valley region. Whether you're searching for your first home, upgrading to fit a growing family, or investing in property, our team of experienced agents provides personalized service, expert market insight, and a commitment to integrity at every step. We take pride in connecting our clients to the communities they love, offering homes that match their lifestyle, budget, and long-term goals.

At Fox Valley Realty, we believe that buying or selling a home should be an exciting and stress-free experience. That’s why we combine cutting-edge digital tools with a warm, hometown approach to deliver results that matter. From professional photography and virtual tours to skillful negotiations and closing guidance, our team ensures every detail is handled with care. With Fox Valley Realty, you’re not just making a move—you’re finding a place to truly call home.
